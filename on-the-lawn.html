On the Lawn

Dairy Air

Artist: Michael Collver
Date: 2014
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 120 x 180 cm

Fabricated from discarded steel mechanisms typical of rural 20th Century life, this piece plays with the latest drone craze wishing its three scavenged rotors could be line her milk to market.

Michael is a STEM educator with Montgomery County Public Schools, Husband, father of four, gardener and an amateur artist dabbling from time to time in steel, stone and clay. His work can be found "grazing" on a half dozen farms around Montgomery County! His work has also featured at the VT Hahn Horticulture Garden, Miller on Main, The Moss Art Center, and the Montgomery Museum.

Dairy Air is available for purchase.

Parallel Universes

Artist: Diane Relf
Fabricator: Dave Angle

Created from rejected and discarded materials no longer seen as useful for commercial application, this piece explores the need for reuse and redesign for sustainability and the potential for many different approaches for addressing human's voracious appetite for the earth's resources.
