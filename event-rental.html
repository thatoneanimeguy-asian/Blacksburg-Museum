Event Rental

The Blacksburg Museum & Cultural Foundation offers a variety of options for your event needs. Combining the elegance of Victorian architecture with the convenience of modern amenities, it is the ideal location for receptions, dinners, presentations, corporate meetings, seminars, small to medium weddings and/or receptions, and reunions. We also provide discounts on rentals at certain membership levels.

WEDDINGS • SPECIAL EVENTS • MEETINGS

Meeting Space

A meeting space for your business or organization. Screen access with wifi and HDMI hookup. Nonprofit organizations may use this space free of charge.

Great Hall

Great for seated lectures, conferences, workshops, and small receptions. Projector and screen access with wifi and HDMI hookup. The back foyer may be used for food.

The Lawn

Perfect for a garden party, wedding, or tailgating. Room for up to two 30x30 tents and other tent configurations are possible. Restroom access and porches are included.

The Alexander Black House

Elegant space for a small dinner, wedding reception, fundraiser, or meeting. Projector and screen access with wifi and HDMI hookup. The back foyer and outdoor areas are included.

Please complete our Rental Event Interest Form so that we can assist you in choosing the best space for your needs!
