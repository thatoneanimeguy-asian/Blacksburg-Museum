We are the stewards of two museums near Blacksburg

The Alexander Black House and St. Luke & Odd Fellows Hall

Our mission: To preserve, interpret, and promote Blacksburg's art, history, and cultural heritage

🏠 The Alexander Black House

Many features, such as rotating exhibitions, programs and events, children's room, gift shop, library and genealogy center, Alexander Black bedroom display, outdoor exhibitions, education and workshop room, staff offices, and much more!

  • Programs & events
  • Children's room & gift shop
  • Library & genealogy center
  • Alexander Black bedroom display
  • Outdoor exhibitions
  • Education & workshop room

⛪ St. Luke & Odd Fellows Hall

The last remaining New Town building, which was a neighborhood that grew amid a racially divided climate. Its residents didn't have the same resources as Blacksburg's white population, so they built a stable community in response to create meaningful lives for themselves.

  • Historic landmark
  • Community legacy
  • Cultural heritage programs
  • Educational exhibits
  • Preserving New Town history
