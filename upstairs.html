Upstairs

Charlie Brouwer Exhibit – Into the Trees

Into the Trees is currently on exhibit in our back stairwell and consists of charcoal drawings of impressively large trees.

As Charlie says, "... A further understanding of the story trees were telling me began with a logging operation in a large nearby wood. I walked among the slashed remains of the forest and made drawings of what I could see. The tragedy, the long-suffering resilience and the small beginnings of hope for recovery I saw there, seemed to echo the human condition. We and the trees are one…we share with them a story of beauty, tragedy, and resilience."
